WHAT DOES A;SA DO?

SUPPORT GROUPS

A;SA can offer support groups for parents, caregivers and individuals with autism to connect and share experiences, resources and information.

SOCIAL SKILLS

Individuals with autism often struggle with social skills and may benefit from social skills groups to learn and practices appropriate behaviors and interactions.

ADVOCACY AND AWARENESS

A;SA can advocate for the rights and needs of individuals with autism and raise awareness about the disorder in the community through public education campaigns, events and media.

OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY

Occupational therapy can help individuals with autism develop skills needed for daily living, such as self care, motor skills and sensory processing.

SPEECH THERAPY

Many individuals with autism struggle with communication and may benefit from speech therapy to improve their language and communication skills.
